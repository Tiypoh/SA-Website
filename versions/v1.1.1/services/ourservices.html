Oil Changes

Our oil changes start at $80 for any passenger car (trucks and diesels exempt), however we are able to source any upgraded oils and filters at your request. We use an advanced pneumatic extractor to suction your oil and guarantee zero mess.

Labor Rate

Sebring Automotive offers a very competitive $80/hour labor rate, in a world where no shop charges less than $100/hour and most dealerships ask you to fork out over $200 for an hour of their time.

Engine Swaps

We are able to swap any motor on any car for the right price, talk to us about sourcing a new motor for your car, whether you blew the last one and you just need it running, or you want to LS swap the world, we got you!

Performance Work

Sebring Automotive may be a mechanic shop but our heart lies with horsepower and modifications. Talk to us about how to get the most out of your ride, we can make your dreams come true.

Classic Cars

Do you have a classic car that nobody is familiar with anymore? We can work on it! We love classic cars and restoration work, bringing pieces of history back to life.

Other
